Background

Experience and interests

Professional background

My background includes project delivery experience across commercial, hospitality, and residential work, along with research focused on construction productivity and procurement. I created this portfolio as part of my AI in Design and Construction course to document how I engaged with course ideas and applied them to AEC-related contexts.

Across the semester, I examined how AI tools can support information synthesis, communication, workflow improvement, and task efficiency in construction-related work.

Why AI in AEC interests me

I am interested in how AI can be used in ways that are practical, useful, and grounded in real project work. For me, the value of AI lies in helping professionals reduce repetitive effort, improve access to information, and strengthen communication while still relying on human oversight and domain knowledge.

This course helped me think more critically about both the opportunities and the limitations of AI in design and construction, especially in relation to adoption, trust, and practical implementation.
