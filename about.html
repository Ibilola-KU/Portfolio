Professional introduction

I created this portfolio as part of my AI in Design and Construction course. My interest in this area comes from the growing role of digital tools in the built environment and the need to understand how AI can be used thoughtfully and effectively in practice.

Across the semester, I examined how AI tools can support idea generation, communication, information synthesis, and task efficiency in design and construction-related work.

Why this course matters to me

This course gave me the opportunity to look more closely at both the promise and the limits of AI in AEC contexts. It helped me think about practical use cases, responsible adoption, and how these tools may affect future workflows in design and construction.

This website serves as a record of that work through project examples, weekly news analysis, and course-related reflections.
